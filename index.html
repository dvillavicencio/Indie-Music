Indie Music

One topic that I find interesting in particular, for example, is discovering Indie Artists and listening to their music. I have spent hours upon hours searching for good Indie Music on popular music platforms such as Spotify. Recently, I have listening to two very good artists named Roosevelt and Cannons. Both of them make great music and their styles have captivated me enough to listen to their songs on repeat for almost a month now.

Roosevelt's latest album has drawn my attention because of its groovy 80s-90s style, sometimes it sounds like is a band playing, nonetheless, he makes the music all by himself! He has, however, stated that although he composes the music on the studio all by himself, his music is supposed to give band-vibes. This can be further exemplified on his live shows where he is not the only one present on stage.
